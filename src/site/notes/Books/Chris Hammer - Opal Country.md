## Opal Country

## My thoughts & notable quotes

You can basically feel the red sand in your shoes and the tiredness in your bones. The main characters are well-defined and written with distinct voices, with some of the side characters fleshed out nicely as well. 
I would have liked this quality for the whole cast, but that's a tall requirement. The story itself sets up intriguing mysteries and comes to a satisfying conclusion, while leaving enough crumbs as a setup for the next story.
